Structure

Steel(JSW)

Aggregates - 20mm & 40mm

The foundation will be as per the structural drawings. Minimum 5 feet deep foundation will be provided.

Solid cement blocks shall be used for outer and partition walls as defined in specification.

The blocks used shall be sound and have undamaged edges and shall be dipped in water before wall construction.

PCC of minimum 125 mm to be provided below ground floor flooring

Internal plastering, external plastering and ceiling plastering shall be done in 1:4 cement mortar.

The thickness of plaster shall be minimum 12mm applied in 2 coats.

Chicken mesh shall be used near beam, column and masonry wall junction.

(Note - Chicken Mesh helps in minimizing the cracks that arise at joints.)

Membrane door/flush door used for bathroom shall be waterproof and bathroom doors should be provided with the handle lock without keys.

Front door stopper should be provided for main door and room doors.

The timber used for windows or doors shall be well seasoned and free from knots, warps, sap, cracks and other defects. All joints should be strong, accurately fitted and glued. All sal wood works is provided with two coats ofpaint.

External walls shall be painted with one coat of primer and two coats ofpaint.

Enamel paint shall be provided for all the SS grill works. All SS painting shall be provided with a anti corrosion coat beforepainting

Ceramic tiles for walls: Tiles shall be free from cracks, spots, grazes, chipped edges and corners. Variation in size shall be limited to +/- 1.5 mm. Thickness shall be as specified, but in no case shall they be less than 6mm.

Wall tiles shall be fixed with tile adhesive. Joints shall be cleaned thoroughly and grouted with site madegrout

Underground sump shall be provided with block masonry. PCC bed concrete of minimum 125mm thickness shall be provided at the bottom. PVC/ Stone footsteps shall be provided for maintenance work of sump. Water Proofing chemical be applied to mortar forplastering.
