About Us

Brick&Bolt has started with the aim of making the construction and renovation experience simplified and trustful.
With our research and experience, we found there is severe trust deficit in this sector and this whole process is quite painful from the customer endpoint. What we were always sure was, that this can’t be fixed by just being an aggregator and hence we have gone way ahead to fix this whole ecosystem to provide most delightful experience to the customer.

This is what we contemplated :
  • Can we take away the project management pain ?
  • Can we make sure that there are NO project delays ?
  • Can we provide the best price to customer ?
  • Can we fix work quality issues ?
  • Can we fix the grey areas in understanding the requirements ?
  • Can we make sure customer pays only when job is done ?
We have come up with the unique proposition which consist of - Online Contract, Payment Management and Facilitation Service and Our Guarantee. Please refer to various sections on website/app to understand the details or contact us for any info.
In addition to the above, We have on-boarded the best professionals (contrcators & renovators) so that you get the most trustful experience at most reasonable cost. At Brick&Bolt, we partner with our customer till the project is complete. Our software and apps help in this direction by making sure that the project data is fed to our systems and we track the project behind the scenes.

So why wait. Lets start. Because WE BUILD AND TRACK JUST LIKE YOU DO !
